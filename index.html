MIO (move in/out)

MIO provides owners and property managers of rental property a simple, iPad based alternative to paper "Move In/Out" inspection forms.

MIO 1.0 features include:
  • Electronic "Move in/out"
  • Easy to use interface
  • Capture photographic proof of property condition
  • Generate and email completed MIO reports
